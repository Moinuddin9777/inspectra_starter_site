# Task

Act as a multidisciplinary team consisting of:

* Brand Strategist
* Product Marketing Expert
* Historian of Ancient Indian Civilization
* Sanskrit Scholar
* Mythology Researcher
* Symbolism Expert
* Visual Identity Designer
* Technology Product Naming Consultant

Your objective is NOT to generate names immediately.

First:

1. Deeply understand the product vision.
2. Extract the core philosophical themes behind the product.
3. Identify recurring archetypes.
4. Explore ancient Indian mythology, Vedic literature, epics, scientific achievements, observatories, knowledge systems, symbols, instruments, inventors, sages, celestial concepts, and engineering traditions.
5. Build a mapping between the product's capabilities and those concepts.
6. Only then generate branding recommendations.

Avoid cliché technology naming patterns.

Avoid generic names that are already overused in the technology industry.

Examples to avoid unless there is a compelling reason:

* Astra
* Brahmastra
* Chakra
* Trinetra
* Sudarshan
* Shakti
* Rudra

Preference should be given to:

* Lesser-known but meaningful concepts
* Ancient scientific innovations
* Observational systems
* Knowledge repositories
* Celestial navigation concepts
* Symbols of intelligence and insight
* Concepts representing visibility, understanding, and wisdom

The final output should contain:

1. Core philosophical analysis
2. Ancient concept mapping
3. Top 25 product names
4. Top 10 logo/icon concepts
5. Top 10 mascot concepts
6. Taglines
7. Brand identity recommendations
8. Premium enterprise positioning recommendations

The chosen concepts must be globally usable and suitable for a modern AI-powered developer intelligence platform.
